About
This game was made by a type lover, for type lovers. If you care about kerning, spend way too long choosing typefaces, or pause on websites just to check the fonts, you’re in the right place.
This game is about three things: having zen-like fun with fonts, learning and mastering typography, and discovering your next favorite typeface. One you might use in your own projects.
Every font in this game is open source. If you see a font you like while playing, you can bookmark it, get a download link, and use it in personal and commercial projects. Free of charge.
The FONT CLASH collection contains almost 200 unique typefaces coming from respected designers and foundries such as The League of Moveable Type, Production Type, Collletttivo, TypeTogether, ArrowType, Noir Blanc Rouge, and of course Google Fonts. The fonts included here are open source releases from these sources.
This is a highly curated selection. Every font was chosen with care. No filler. Only high-quality type worth knowing.
Every font in this game is released under the SIL Open Font License (OFL). This allows you to use the fonts in personal and commercial projects, as long as the license terms are respected.
Found a mistake, a wrong classification, or a font that should be here? I’d love to hear from you. Reach out at silvan@silvanfey.com.
