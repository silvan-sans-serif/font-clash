About
This game was made by a font lover, for a font lover. If you obsess over kerning, spend twenty minutes picking the right typeface, or open a website just to check what font they're using — you are home.
Found a mistake, a wrong classification, or a font that deserves to be here? I'd genuinely love to know. Drop me a line at hello@typelover.example.com.
Every font in SORTS is a Google Font. Without that, licensing a collection of this quality would run into tens of thousands of dollars. So yes — Google Fonts makes this possible, and it makes it free for you.
That said, as a typography nerd I want to be clear: Google Fonts is genuinely good. There is real craft in that library. The families in this game were hand-picked one by one. Not random filler — a curated selection of faces worth knowing.
SORTS is also a tool for discovery. Spot a face you like mid-game? Hit the ⓘ on the completed zone, then head straight to Google Fonts and snatch it for your next web or design project.
